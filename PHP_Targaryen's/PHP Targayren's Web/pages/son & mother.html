THE SON'S REFLECTION

-ART BY AYOMA WIERATHNA-

Crafting Artificial Intelligence

LKR 14,000.00


Grounded Memories: A Son's Reflection" is a deeply introspective journey into the heart and soul of a son as he grapples with the absence of his beloved mother.
Through the prism of his memories, he rediscovers the profound impact she had on shaping his identity and guiding his path. Each cherished moment becomes a beacon of light in the darkness of grief, reminding him of her unwavering love and enduring legacy. As he tenderly tends to the garden of their shared experiences, he finds comfort in the roots they've planted together, knowing that her spirit lives on in the depths of his being. This poignant narrative celebrates the beauty of their bond, honoring the profound connection that transcends the physical realm and continues to blossom in the fertile soil of his heart.
