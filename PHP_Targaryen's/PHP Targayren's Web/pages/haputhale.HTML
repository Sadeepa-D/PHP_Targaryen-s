STARRY NIGHT OVER HAPUTHALE

-Photography by Thilina Kaluthotage-

Thilina Kaluthotage is a talented photographer known for his captivating and evocative imagery

LKR 15,000.00


On the tranquil night of February 18, 2024, the celestial canvas above Haputale, Sri Lanka, unveiled a mesmerizing display of the Milky Way. As if painted with stardust, the Milky Way gracefully arched across the sky, casting its luminous glow over the picturesque viewpoint. Beneath its cosmic embrace, the earthly landscape faded into the background, allowing the majestic beauty of the galaxy to take center stage. This enchanting scene invited onlookers to contemplate the infinite wonders of the universe, filling hearts with a sense of awe and wonderment.
