Paint of the Week

Marco Ortolan is an Argentine artist (draftsman, painter) who started out as an impressionist painter. He moved through hyperrealism, and today, generally in the language of realism, his techniques are highly personal. His work is finished with plastic that gives a unique impression. He believes that an artist must be universal, and today his techniques are loosely expressed, with plasticity and impressionistic tendencies. The process starts with loose drawings assembled in a grid, and the plasticity begins to dominate the scene, adapting to the artist's demands.
