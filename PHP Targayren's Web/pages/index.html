Paint of the Week

Marco Ortolan is an Argentine artist (draftsman, painter) who started out as an impressionist painter (the school in which he began painting), moved through hyperrealism, and today, generally in the language of realism, is highly personal. Finished with plastic that gives a unique impression. It does not preclude entry into other themes, such as ``the artist (as a painter) must be universal'' (which supports Leonardo da Vinci's idea in his theory of painting). Today, his techniques are loosely expressed, with plasticity and impressionistic tendencies, which emerge through the process of each composition, such as scraping with a spatula or scratching to incorporate the tones of the support. The process begins with loose drawings assembled in a grid to solidify the proportions, then the first tones emerge and from there the plasticity begins to dominate the scene back and forth until it is adapted to the same artist's demands.

Welcome to Leesa Art Gallery

At Leesa Art Gallery, we're passionate about connecting art lovers with exceptional contemporary works from around the globe. Founded in 2024, our mission is to champion artists, promote creativity, and foster a vibrant art community.

Our Vision

To be a leading online destination for discovering and acquiring contemporary art that enriches lives and transcends boundaries.

Our Collection

Explore our carefully curated selection of paintings, sculptures, photography, and digital art, each chosen for its artistic excellence and originality.

Our Commitment

We're dedicated to supporting emerging and established artists, providing them with a platform to showcase their talent and connect with collectors worldwide.

Customer Experience

We prioritize your satisfaction and offer a seamless browsing and purchasing experience. Our team is here to assist you every step of the way.

Community Engagement

Join us in fostering meaningful connections within the art community through virtual exhibitions, artist talks, and collaborations with cultural organizations.
